Screen 1 — State notes
·Rota unlocked: Amber border on frequency card + warning row replaces green rota lock status. "Process payroll →" remains enabled.
·Rota locked: Weekly card shows green lock confirmation. Process payroll button is greyed/disabled until rota is locked on the locked-rota journey.
·Story 1 trigger: Clicking "Process payroll →" on an unlocked rota card launches the warning modal — not the payroll calculation directly.
·No silent processing: The system never processes silently past an unlocked rota — the warning modal is mandatory.

Screen 6 — Payroll summary notes
·Amendment column: Only employees with a rota adjustment show a value in the Amendment column. Unaffected employees (e.g. Anna Clarke) show a dash — no visual noise for unaffected rows.
·Label on the row: "Adj — Jan 2026" beneath the amount immediately communicates which period the adjustment relates to without opening the payslip.
·Positive & negative: Green pill for additions, red pill for removals/reductions — mirrors the comparison modal colour language.
·Gross reflects adjustment: The gross column is the sum of basic pay, other elements, and the amendment — Kevin Hart's £2,184 + £364 = £2,548 gross.
